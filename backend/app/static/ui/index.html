CONDITIONS WE DETECT

Our deep learning model is trained to identify six common skin conditions with high accuracy, providing preliminary insights to support informed decision-making.

1

Atopic Dermatitis

Chronic, itchy inflammation of the skin, often associated with allergies.

2

Normal Skin

Healthy skin without visible signs of disease or irritation.

3

Melanoma

A serious type of skin cancer that develops from pigment-producing cells.

4

Seborrheic Keratoses

Common noncancerous skin growth that can look like a wart or mole.

5

Nail Fungus

A common condition that begins as a white or yellow spot under the tip of your fingernail or toenail.

6

Chickenpox

A highly contagious viral infection causing an itchy, blister-like rash on the skin.
